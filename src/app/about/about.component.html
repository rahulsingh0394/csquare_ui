Let's talk services

We started our journey with providing tuition classes in 2014. It was going perfect then after 2 years we realized with this way we will not be able to create BIG CHANGE!
As we belive that education in India requires many modification, so we came up with an idea of Home Tuition and Online Tuition.
We are looking forward to bring more changes as we will be providing videos for students so that they can understand concept very well and in case if they miss some topic, they can get video for the same which ultimately leads to much more understanding. We here at CsquareEducation are not working on creating more and more human labour or say educated labour but we are trying to bring creative learning for student where they not only follow order but use their creativity to learn and adapt the skillset. As per our opinion students are not getting eductaion upto mark as today also our education system is to fulfill requirement of industrial labour who just follow order and aren't allowed to use their own creativity. So we are bound to bring back the creativty of every child in India back so that they can decide their own future but not our education system.

Csquareeducation Established in 2014 and from that day, we are a zero revenue generators and we seriously entered in to the Home Tuition business effectively from June, 2015.
We have started our company as training institute and slowly we have entered into home tuition. Initial days we have involved mostly on training class, but slowly Home Tuition business started getting more and more revenue and very good response from client. From June,2015 onwards we have started focusing more on home tuition and this home tuition become primary business for us. We are proving home tuition in all over Bangalore from Nursery – to – Graduation along with all kind of board and Universities. We are having tutors from 1 year – to – 20+ years of experience. As part of enrollment policy all tutors are verified by CsquareEducation and we are keeping their documents. Parents and tutor both are engaged in contract with Csquareeducation before staring home tuition and we are responsible to take care of their issues and concern till they are associated with us.

We successfully completed 1800 Parental clients and over 5000 Tutors from all over the Bangalore at today’s date. The rate of success is growing day by day and this is the best time to expand our business. Where I have seen 100% result in the 2015 and very Extreme result of 200% Business has increased in 2016-17.
